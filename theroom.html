The Shoebox





You thought there would be a pair of jordans under this shoebox? What I keep here are notes and letters from friends that I have received since I would say the start of college. So I would say about 2010. Maybe this is just something about me but I like to cherish memories. It's somebody's words trapped in time. I have letters that were sent to me while I studied abroad in college. I studied abroad in El Salvador and Denmark. The letters are frozen in time which helps me reminisce where I was during that stage in my life.

I have letters from friends while I was in the following places.

  • El Salvador
  • Denmark
  • Ecuador
  • France

But what I cherish the most is the affirmations letters I received throughout events I have worked in. I have worked with many colleges and high schools during my time with Rostro as well as my time at Saint Joseph's University. It shows moments of love and thankfulness during a particular trip. Honestly, during times of loneliness or feeling down, I have looked at these notes and go deep into the memories and remember moments of despair and happiness.
